## What is Horizon?

Horizon solves a real problem. Migrant workers send $800 billion per year back home. Traditional services like Western Union charge 3вЂ“7% fees and take 1вЂ“3 days. Horizon uses the Stellar blockchain to make it instant and nearly free.

**Example:** Joy works in Dubai and wants to send money to his mother in Mumbai.
- Traditional: Rs.200вЂ“500 fee, 1вЂ“3 days
- Horizon: Rs.1.80 fee (0.1 XLM), 5вЂ“10 seconds

---

## Problem Statement
 
Every month, millions of migrant workers send money home to their families across borders. The current reality:
 
| Problem | Reality |
|---------|---------|
| Bank transfer fee | в‚№150вЂ“500 per transfer |
| Transfer time | 1вЂ“3 business days |
| No bank account access | 1.4 billion people unbanked worldwide |
| Western Union / MoneyGram | 5вЂ“8% fee deducted from poor families |
| Hidden exchange rate cuts | Additional 2вЂ“3% loss |
 
**Horizon solves this.** Send money in under 10 seconds for less than в‚№2 вЂ” no bank account required.
 
---
 
## Solution
 
Horizon is a mobile-first cross-border remittance dApp built on Stellar Blockchain:
 
- Sender scans receiver's QR code в†’ sends money in under 10 seconds
- Receiver gets instant in-app notification and sees balance in local currency
- Pay anyone by scanning their QR code
- Withdraw cash from ATM using a 6-digit code
- No bank account needed вЂ” no crypto knowledge required
- Fee: Less than в‚№2 per transfer

## Advanced Feature: Fee Sponsorship
 
Horizon implements **Stellar Fee Bump Transactions** for gasless receiving:
 
- Receivers pay **zero XLM fees** when receiving money
- Horizon's fee sponsor account covers all network fees
- Toggle available on the Send page: `Gasless Transfer (Fee Sponsored)`
- Implemented via `StellarSdk.TransactionBuilder.buildFeeBumpTransaction()`

## Transaction Flow

```
1. Sender selects receiver
   в†’ Search by name (from registered users)
   в†’ Scan QR code with camera
   в†’ Paste Stellar wallet address

2. Sender enters amount in their local currency
   в†’ e.g., 100 AED

3. App converts using live rates
   в†’ 100 AED = 27.20 USD = 247.27 XLM

4. Sender confirms and clicks Send
   в†’ Freighter wallet signs the transaction
   в†’ Submitted to Stellar Testnet

5. Settlement in 5вЂ“10 seconds

6. Receiver gets notified within 8 seconds
   в†’ Red badge on History tab
   в†’ Toast popup: "Money Received! +247.27 XLM"

7. Sender gets receipt
   в†’ Shows all transfer details
   в†’ Save as image or share on WhatsApp
```
