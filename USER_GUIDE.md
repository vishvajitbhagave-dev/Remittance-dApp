# Horizon — User Guide

> Complete guide for using Horizon cross-border remittance app

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1 — Open the App
Go to: **https://remittance-d-app.vercel.app**

### Step 2 — Install Freighter Wallet
1. Go to https://freighter.app
2. Click **Add to Chrome**
3. Follow setup instructions
4. Create a new wallet (save your secret phrase safely)
5. Switch to **Testnet** in Freighter settings

### Step 3 — Create Your Account
1. Click **Create Account**
2. Fill in your name, phone number, country
3. Complete KYC verification (select ID type, enter ID number)
4. Connect your Freighter wallet
5. Enter the OTP sent to your phone

### Step 4 — Fund Your Wallet
1. After login, click **Fund Testnet** button
2. Your wallet gets 10,000 free XLM (test money)
3. Wait 5 seconds for balance to appear

---

## Sending Money

### Option 1 — Search Contact
1. Click **Send** tab
2. Click **Search Contact**
3. Type the receiver's name or phone number
4. Select from results
5. Enter amount and currency
6. Click **Send**

### Option 2 — Scan QR Code
1. Click **Send** tab
2. Click **Scan QR Code**
3. Allow camera permission
4. Point camera at receiver's QR code
5. Address auto-fills — enter amount and send

### Option 3 — Paste Wallet Address
1. Click **Send** tab
2. Click **Paste Wallet Address**
3. Paste the receiver's Stellar address (starts with G)
4. Enter amount and currency
5. Click **Send**

### Fee Sponsorship (Gasless)
- Toggle **Fee Sponsorship** ON before sending
- Your transaction fee (0.1 XLM) will be sponsored
- You pay zero fee

---

## Receiving Money

1. Click **Receive** tab
2. Show your QR code to the sender
3. Or copy your wallet address and share it
4. Money arrives in 5–10 seconds
5. You get a notification automatically

### Download Your QR Code
1. Click **Receive** tab
2. Click **Download QR**
3. Save the image to your phone
4. Share via WhatsApp or any app

---

## Transaction History

1. Click **History** tab
2. Search transactions by memo, date, or amount
3. Filter: **All / Sent / Received**
4. Click **View** on any transaction to see it on Stellar Explorer

### Download History
1. Click **Download History** button
2. Select year and format (PDF or CSV)
3. Click **Download**

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|---------|
| Balance not showing | Click Fund Testnet or wait 10 seconds |
| Transaction failed | Check you have enough XLM (minimum 1.1 XLM) |
| Freighter not connecting | Refresh page, check Freighter is on Testnet |
| QR scan not working | Allow camera permission in browser settings |
| Search not finding user | Both users must be on same browser/device |
