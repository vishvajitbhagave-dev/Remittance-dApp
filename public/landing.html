How to Send Money to India
Using Horizon — Instant & Under Rs.2

Whether you are sending from UAE, USA, UK, or anywhere in the world — Horizon delivers your money to India in under 10 seconds on the Stellar blockchain.

1

Create Your Horizon Account

Go to remittance-d-app.vercel.app and click Create New Account. Enter your full name, phone number, email address, and select your country. This takes under 3 minutes.

2

Complete KYC Verification

Upload your identity document — Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Driving License, or Voter ID. Horizon validates your document instantly with field-level verification.

3

Connect Freighter Wallet

Install the Freighter browser extension from freighter.app. Connect it to Horizon and click Fund Testnet to receive free XLM for testing. Your Stellar address is auto-linked to your account.

4

Find Your Receiver in India

Click Send and search for your receiver by name. Or scan their QR code from the Receive tab. Or paste their Stellar wallet address directly. All three methods work instantly.

5

Enter Amount in Your Currency

Enter the amount you want to send in your local currency — AED, USD, GBP, or any of the 100+ supported currencies. Horizon instantly shows the exact INR amount the receiver will get.

6

Confirm — Money Arrives in India

Click Send and approve in Freighter. Within 5–10 seconds, your family in India receives the money and gets an instant notification. Fee charged: less than Rs.2. That's it.
